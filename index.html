About

Yo Maps, born Elton Mulenga in Kasama, Zambia, is one of the country's best-known singer-songwriters. His sound blends Afro-pop, R&B, and polished Zambian pop songwriting into records built for radio and streaming.

He first broke through with Finally, and his Spotify catalog now includes albums like Komando, Try Again, and My Hero. Popular songs such as Bana Pwanya, Aye, Lwambo, Mr & Mrs, and I Wanna Eat show the range that keeps him at the center of Zambia's modern music conversation.

Career Notes

Yo Maps — Profile Notes

Born Elton Mulenga in Kasama, Yo Maps built his name through melodic songwriting, clean production, and a style that connects across generations of Zambian listeners.

From Finally to Komando, Try Again, and My Hero, his catalog shows a steady run of polished releases and collaborations that keep him front and center in the country's pop landscape.
